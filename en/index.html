Hotel

Built in the iconic 50's art-deco style, Le Gédéon offers 12 beautiful rooms just seconds from the beach.

You'll love the calm atmosphere of our family run hotel, where we offer you the chance to relax and enjoy our charming service in French or English.

Have a peaceful night's rest in our calm location and wake up to the gentle sounds of the Mediterranean.

What more do you need? Apart from a delicious breakfast on our sunny terrace!

Restaurant

We source all our food and wines locally and personnally so that you can enjoy the best seasonal produce of the area.

Creativity is the key to our healthy and delicious tapas style menu. Why not try our amazing 'mi-cuit de saumon', or cuttlefish, sardines, or king prawns grilled perfectly and served 'a la plancha'?

And just in case you have room, Patissier Cedric's desserts are out of this world!

Pair your meal with one of our personally selected wines from the region, for example the organic Galets Dorés Château Mourgues du Grés.

Leisure and fun

Whether you're here to ramp up or slow down, the beach is seconds away and has everything you need!

You can rent one of our stand up paddle boards and hit the Med. Just let us know if you'll need an end of day massage and we'll book it for you.

For a calmer life, just pick up a beach towel from reception and hit the beach for the day - lunch in our restaurant is only 30 metres' walk back!

A very pleasant stay on the seaside!

Just 30 metres from the beach, Le Gedeon is open from March to November. The bustling Carnon Harbour is only a few minutes' drive from our calm, home from home hospitality. Our 12 lovely rooms offer all the flexibility you need with double, triple accomodation, family and connecting rooms. And many of our rooms have a view onto the Mediterranean.

"We spent a week the 4 together as a family at the begining of July. We received a warm welcome and enjoyed the very good food well adjusted in period of heatwave! Rooms are simple, fully equipped and beautiful." - Charlotte Montréal
